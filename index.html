The passion that drives Panache

Annual Cultural Fest

Panache, our much-anticipated annual cultural fest, serves as a vibrant gathering that marks a significant highlight in our academic calendar. It brings together students, faculty, and staff to celebrate the richness of our diverse cultural tapestry.

Diverse Daily Themes

Each day of Panache unfolds with a kaleidoscope of themes, offering a captivating journey through various cultural expressions. From the glitz and glamour of Bollywood Day to the communal unity on Group Day, each theme is carefully curated to engage and delight participants.

Vibrant Community Spirit

Panache transcends an event, epitomizing our college's vibrant spirit. Fostering togetherness, it's a platform for students to express creativity, showcase talents, and forge lasting connections in an energetic atmosphere. Panache pulsates with the enthusiasm that defines our close-knit community..
